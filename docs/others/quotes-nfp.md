## Attribution notes

A few quotes carry debated attributions worth noting for accuracy in your app:

- **Quote 38** ("Between stimulus and response…") is widely attributed to Viktor Frankl but researchers have not located it in his published works. It was popularized by Stephen Covey in connection with Frankl's philosophy. Consider attributing as "Attributed to Viktor Frankl."
- **Quote 56** ("We are what we repeatedly do…") was written by historian Will Durant summarizing Aristotle's *Nicomachean Ethics*, not by Aristotle himself. Universally attributed to Aristotle in popular usage.
- **Quote 34** ("For what it's worth: it's never too late…") originates from the 2008 film *The Curious Case of Benjamin Button* (screenplay by Eric Roth), not from Fitzgerald's original writing. Universally attributed to Fitzgerald online.
- **Quote 41** ("Success is not final, failure is not fatal…") is commonly attributed to Churchill but has not been verified in his authenticated writings.
- **Quote 24** ("I am not what happened to me…") is popularly associated with Carl Jung, though the exact phrasing is debated among scholars.
