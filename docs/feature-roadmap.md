# Feature Roadmap — 90 Days NoFap Timer

Features ranked by impact on **user retention and acquisition**. Each section answers the question: *why would someone open this app tomorrow?*

---

## Priority 1 — Retention (Why Come Back Daily)

### Daily Check-In Ritual
- One-tap daily commitment when opening the app ("I'm staying strong today")
- Unlocks the daily quote only after check-in (creates habit loop)
- Streak badge for consecutive check-in days (separate from sobriety streak)
- Miss a check-in → gentle reminder notification ("Day X — check in?")

### Home Screen Widget
- Live countdown timer visible without opening the app
- Small widget: days + hours
- Large widget: full stats (streak, total clean days, today's quote snippet)
- iOS and Android support

### Daily Notifications
- Configurable reminder time ("Your daily check-in is ready")
- Milestone approach warnings ("3 days until your 30-day milestone!")
- Milestone celebration push ("🎉 You hit 30 days!")
- Motivational morning nudge with a snippet of today's quote

### Streak Milestones — Expanded
- Current milestones: 7, 14, 30, 60, 90 days
- Add: 1, 3, 100, 180, 365 days
- Each milestone unlocks a badge stored in a personal "trophy shelf"
- Share milestone card to social media or messages (optional)

---

## Priority 2 — Depth (Why Keep Using Past 90 Days)

### Personal Best Mode
- Track longest streak ever achieved
- "Beat your record" goal displayed alongside 90-day goal
- History graph showing all previous streak lengths
- Message when current streak beats personal best: "New record!"

### Phases Beyond 90 Days
- **Phase 1** (0–30 days): Foundation
- **Phase 2** (31–60 days): Building
- **Phase 3** (61–90 days): Mastery
- **Phase 4** (90–180 days): Sustaining
- **Phase 5** (180+ days): Freedom
- Each phase has its own color theme, badge, and quote category emphasis

### Trigger Pattern Insights
- Weekly summary of logged triggers (e.g., "You've noted Stress 3 times this week")
- Day-of-week breakdown: "Your resets most often happen on weekends"
- Time-of-day pattern: "Late night is your most common trigger"
- Actionable nudge based on patterns (e.g., Sunday afternoon reminder)

### Journal / Notes
- Short daily note (280 chars) attached to each calendar day
- Viewable by tapping a calendar cell
- Exported as PDF timeline of your journey

---

## Priority 3 — Acquisition (Why Someone Downloads This Over Others)

### Accountability Partner
- Share a private link with one trusted person
- They see your streak (days only, no other data)
- They can send a one-tap encouragement ("Proud of you 💪")
- You see their encouragement as a banner notification
- No social feed — purely 1:1 and private

### Beautiful Milestone Share Cards
- Auto-generated image when you hit a milestone
- Clean design: "30 days strong" with streak count and start date
- No app branding that feels spammy — subtle, shareable
- Easy "Share" button after milestone modal

### Onboarding "Why" Step
- Before date picker: ask why the user is starting
- Options: "More energy", "Better focus", "Relationship goals", "Self-respect", etc.
- Personalizes quote categories toward their stated reason
- Displayed back to them on hard days ("Remember why you started: Better focus")

### App Store Optimization Features
- "Emergency" button: immediate distraction screen (breathing exercise, cold shower reminder, quote)
- Urge surfing timer: 10-minute countdown with progress to "ride out" a craving
- These features appear prominently in App Store screenshots

---

## Priority 4 — Polish (What Makes It Feel Premium)

### Dark / Light / AMOLED Themes
- Auto dark mode (already partially done)
- True black AMOLED option for battery saving
- Optional color accent per phase (blue → green → gold)

### Calendar Enhancements
- Mood color on clean days (optional: rate day 1–5 stars)
- Visual difference between "easy" and "hard" clean days
- Month view toggle (not just 90-cell grid)

### Stats Deep Dive Screen
- Average streak length
- Total time clean (formatted as "X months, Y days")
- Success rate (clean days / total days since start)
- Longest streak vs. current streak comparison bar

### Haptic Themes
- Customize vibration patterns for milestones
- Toggle haptics on/off per event type

### Animated Progress
- Subtle particle animation when progress bar fills
- Confetti burst at each milestone (not just 90 days)
- Calendar cell "fill" animation when day flips to clean

---

## Priority 5 — Monetization (How to Sustain the App)

### Free Tier
- Core timer, basic stats, daily quote
- 90-day calendar view
- Manual trigger logging

### Pro Tier (one-time purchase or $2.99/month)
- Widget support
- Accountability partner feature
- Trigger pattern insights
- Journal / notes
- Milestone share cards
- Phase system (beyond 90 days)
- All future features

### Optional: Community (high effort, high reward)
- Anonymous public leaderboard (opt-in)
- Weekly challenge rooms (e.g., "30 days in February")
- Peer support chat (moderated, anonymous)
- This is the single highest-retention feature if done well
