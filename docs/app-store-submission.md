## Full Description

Starting over is an act of courage, not failure.

90 Days is a simple, private timer built for people who want to break free from porn. No lectures. No shame. Just a clean counter that starts the moment you open the app — and restarts whenever you need it to.

**How it works**

Set your start date when you first open the app and your 90-day journey begins. Check in each day with how you're feeling, log a note if something's on your mind, and watch your progress build — one round at a time. If you have a setback, log it honestly and start a new round. Every round is tracked so you can see how far you've come, not just how far you fell.

No accounts. No data shared. No judgment.

**Why 90 days?**

Research consistently shows it takes around 90 days for the brain to begin rewiring itself away from compulsive habits. This app keeps that goal front and center — one day at a time.

**Built differently**

Most habit apps are built for streaks and guilt. This one is built for recovery. Every setback is information, not a punishment. Every day you complete is a win worth celebrating. Your mood, your notes, your history — all of it stays on your device and nowhere else.

**Features**

- Clean 90-day timer anchored to your chosen start date
- Daily check-in — log your mood (Struggling / Neutral / Strong) and an optional reflection note
- Rotating daily prompts to help you build self-awareness over time
- Insights tab — per-round stats including relapses, longest clean streak, days completed, and a mood breakdown across your check-ins
- History tab — a day-by-day log of your check-ins and relapses with expandable notes
- Multiple rounds — every attempt is saved so you can track your growth across rounds, not just your latest one
- Milestone moments — gentle notifications at 7, 14, 30, and 60 clean days to mark real progress
- Daily reminders — choose morning, afternoon, or evening check-in notifications
- Fully private — everything stays on your device, no account required
- Clean, distraction-free design built for focus, not engagement
